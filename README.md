# ZIYAN

**ZIYAN** is a decentralized protocol that enables users to mint, trade, and earn yield on tokenized equities.

---

## 🌐 Overview

ZIYAN bridges traditional finance and decentralized finance (DeFi) by bringing real-world equities on-chain. Users can seamlessly access tokenized stocks, trade them in a decentralized environment, and generate yield—all without intermediaries.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🪙 **Tokenized Equities**

  * Mint blockchain-based representations of real-world stocks

* 🔄 **Decentralized Trading**

  * Trade tokenized assets permissionlessly

* 📈 **Yield Generation**

  * Earn passive income through DeFi mechanisms

* 🔐 **Non-Custodial**

  * Full control over your assets at all times

* ⚡ **Fast & Scalable**

  * Built for high-performance transactions

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Users mint tokenized equities backed by real-world assets
2. Assets are listed and traded on decentralized markets
3. Liquidity providers and holders earn yield through:

   * Staking
   * Liquidity pools
   * Protocol incentives
